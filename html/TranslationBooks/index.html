About

You can easily read books in foreign languages. When you click on a word or a sentence, you hear their pronunciation and see the translation. Books and texts for reading you choose yourself.

The app supports html pages and epub files.

The app allows you to choose the encoding and font size, and stores a list of books and a reading position.
