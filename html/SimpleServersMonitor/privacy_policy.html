Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Simple Servers Monitor app.

Data Collection

I do not collect, store, sell, or share any personal data from this app.

The app does not send your server list, site list, settings, monitoring results, or other app data to me. I do not operate servers that receive data from the app.

Local Data and Network Checks

Server and site addresses that you add to the app are stored on your device. The app may connect directly from your device to the servers or sites you choose to monitor in order to check their availability. Those requests are made by your device to the addresses you entered and are not sent to me.

Third-Party Services

The app does not currently use third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or cloud services.

Third-party services may be added in a future version. If that happens, this Privacy Policy will be updated to describe what services are used and what data, if any, they process.

Cookies and Tracking

The app does not use cookies or tracking technologies.

Children's Privacy

I do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact me by email: simpleserversmonitorios@gmail.com.
